About Aaghaz

Aaghaz is the Annual Cultural Fest of PGDAV College and is organized with great pomp and show. It's extended over in two days, where each day inculcates numerous competitions of our adept college societies. This year the 16th and 17th of April, 2021 will witness the fall of Aaghaz.

Hyperion takes immense pride to announce that Mr. Sudhir Chaudhary will be the chief guest for Aaghaz 21- Aviral The Voyage Continues.

Mr. Sudhir Chaudhary is an Indian journalist and media personality. He is serving as the editor-in-chief of Zee News and hosts the prime-time show Daily News And Analysis on Zee News. We all know him for his well-researched and comprehensive news analysis segment DNA.

It's a matter of great honor to have such a prominent figure as our Chief Guest.
