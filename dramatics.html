PROP-IT-OFF

SOLO-ACTING COMPETITION

NAVRANG: THE STAGE PLAY SOCIETY OF PGDAV COLLEGE (M)

ABHIVYAKTI’21

MOST CONVERSATIONS ARE SIMPLY MONOLOGUES DELIVERED IN THE PRESENCE OF A WITNESS .NAVRANG- The Stage Play Society of PGDAV College(M)
invites you to a solo-act competition PROP-IT-OFF.Deliver a monologue with the creative use of prop to woo the judges and win exciting prizes .
In the world full of characters , words, gestures, language and many other aspects of theatre where in monoact remains an inseparable part of the theatre.
We are providing you with the opportunity, liberty and freedom to choose your own characters and portrait it in whatever manner you desire to.

Rules and Regulations:

1.Single entries only.
2.A prop from the list should be used in your act. (prop list: watch/clock/calendar /colours).
3.The time limit is 3-5 minutes.
4.No cuts and edits should be there.
5.Video to be submitted through Google photos link.
6.Entries allowed either in Hindi or in English or both.
7.Obscenity and offensive language is not allowed.
8.Plagiarism will lead to disqualification or negative marks.
9.Decision of the judges will be final and abiding.

Judging Criteria:- prop use, diction, creativity and content.

THY REVERT

(SHORT FILM COMPETITION)

NAVRANG: THE STAGE PLAY SOCIETY OF PGDAV COLLEGE (M)

ABHIVYAKTI’21

A story should have a beginning, a middle and an end, but not necessarily in that order.
NAVRANG is hosting a short film competition-"Thy Revert".
Pick up your camera and start shooting and portray the world through your lens.
The participants need to shoot the story in a reverse way but dialogues must make sense in the sequence.

Rules and Regulations:

1.No entry fee.
2.No limited participants.
3.Short film only so no documentaries allowed .
4.Speech may be bilingual(Hindi or English or both)
5.Usage of stock footage is not allowed.
6.The length of the film should be 10-12 minutes.
7.There is no bar on subject matter.
8.The extension to the video should be mp4 or mkv and to be shared through google photos link.
9.Wise selection of words should be kept in mind.

Judging Criteria :flow of the story, expressions and direction.

Write-Camera-Action

A Story Writing cum MonoAct Competition

Rules and Regulations:

1.This is an individual competition
2.The competition will be held in two rounds: Story Writing Round & Mono-Act Round

⩷Round 1- Story Writing
1.Write a short story on any one of the given genres
2.The first two sentences or the last two sentences of your story should specifically give out the genre you have chosen
3.The story should be in English/Hindi
4.Word limit: 500 words
5.Your story should have a title
6.Mention the genre along with the title. For example, "The Thirsty Crow (existentialism)"
7.Submit your story in PDF format

⩷Round 2- MonoAct
1.This round will be held live on 17th April via Zoom/Google Meet
2.Each shortlisted participant will be provided a particular genre along with two sentences from some other participant's story.
3.The participants will prepare a mono-act based on the genre provided.
4.Winners will be getting exciting cash prizes along with e-Certificates

Genre:-

1.Absurd
2.Romance
3.Existentialism
4.Comedy
5.Crime
6.Horror
