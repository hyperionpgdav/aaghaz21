THE DESIGN NEST

Web Development Competition

General Rules:

1. It is an individual Event.
2. Date: 16th April 2021
3. Time: 10:00 A.M.

Round 1:

1. Question and answer round.
2. It will be held on google forms.

Round 2:

Timings: 1:30 P.M. - 4:30 P.M.
1. Create a website using HTML and CSS on the provided topic.
2. Topic will be provided through email on the registered email ID.
3. Participants are allowed to use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MYSQL.
4. Duration - 3 Hours.
5. Website will be judged on the participant's creativity and website's responsiveness.
6. Use of any framework such as Bootstrap and jQuery is restricted..
7. Participants have to submit their website on Google drive whose link will be provided on the mail on the registered email ID along with the topic of website.

Note- It is participant's choice to create Backend. It is not compulsory but extra points will be provided if used.

Round 3:

Timings: 05:00 P.M.
1. It will be held on Google Meet.
2. Selected candidates have to explain their design to the jury and the team and have to answer their queries.

Code Toons

Coding Competition

16th April, 2021 - 11:00 A.M.

Rules and Regulations:

1. It will be an individual coding event.
2. It will consist of 2 rounds.
3. First round will be a qualifying quiz and will be conducted via google forms.
4. Second round will be conducted on meet and will consist of 3 sets of 3 questions each.
5. Participants will be given an hour to attempt the problems and submit the code on Google Classroom.
6. Languages allowed: C++, Java and Python.
7. Participant with the highest number of correct solutions wins.
8. In case of a tie, participant who has attempted questions from maximum number of sets will be declared winner. Time of submission will also be taken account of.
9. Decision of the judges/managing team shall be considered final.
