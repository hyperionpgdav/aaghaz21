Naad

Indian Solo Instrumental Competition

Rules and Regulations:

1. Video limit: 1min - 3min. for both the events. Exceeding time limit will lead to point deduction.
2. The video and audio must not be edited
3. Backing tracks are not allowed. Maximum 2 accompanists allowed.
4. The participant's face must be clearly visible in the video.
5. Genre for solo singing:
Semi-classical, gazal, bhajan, thumri, Bollywood,folk
6. Genre for instrumental:
Classical, Semi-classical
7. There will be two rounds for both the events.
8. Only one video is required for both the rounds.
